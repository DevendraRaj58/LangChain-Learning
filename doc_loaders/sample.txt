The Quiet Forest

Morning sunlight kissed the trees,
Dancing softly with the breeze.
Birds awoke with cheerful song,
Guiding every leaf along.

A little stream began to flow,
Shining where the wildflowers grow.
Butterflies in colors bright,
Painted dreams in golden light.

As the evening hugged the sky,
Stars appeared, both low and high.
The forest whispered, calm and deep,
Inviting every soul to sleep.
